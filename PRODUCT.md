# Product

## Register
brand

## Users
Locals and visitors in Sibalom, Antique who want a quiet, welcoming spot
to enjoy specialty coffee. They value authenticity, a sense of place,
and emotional rest over urban hustle.

## Product Purpose
Communicate what makes Kapé Kanlungan a sanctuary — specialty coffee
rooted in a provincial Filipino setting. Drive foot traffic and social
follows. Success = a visitor feels the warmth before they arrive.

## Brand Personality
Poetic · Rooted · Unhurried

## Anti-references
- Generic café chains (Starbucks-clone aesthetic, generic sans fonts)
- Urban hipster minimalism (black + white, single bold sans)
- AI-cream-beige landing pages
- Formal luxury hotel websites

## Design Principles
1. The place is the brand. Warmth comes from the space's actual materials
   (amber wood, warm light, tropical plants) — not from beige backgrounds.
2. Rest in every element. Nothing fights for attention. Generous space,
   slow pace, one idea per fold.
3. Filipino first. The tagline is in Filipino. The name is in Filipino.
   The aesthetic references the bahay kubo and the Philippine landscape.
4. Poetry over promotion. Copy leads with feeling, not features.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA as baseline. Reduced motion support required.
